Who We Are

24-Form Baking was started just before the COVID-19 pandemic in March of 2020 by Jake "nubby" Lee, based out of San Jose, California. Jake loves baking bread and finds it both meditative and delicious. This baking business began after Jake was foolishly encouraged by some friends to sell some bread. Now he is heavily experimenting to pump out signature and specialty loafs. Stop by and spread the loaf!
